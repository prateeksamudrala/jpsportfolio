About Me

A brief intro - my work and interests

Thank you for stopping by! I usually go by Jyothi in a "formal" context. Some of my close friends and family call me Prateek. My pronouns are he/him/his.

I have had the opportunity of working in Software Development teams, in a Product Management space, for the last 10 + years in startup, growth-stage and large organizations.

Presently, I work as a Senior Product Manager at a growth-stage, marketing automation company. Here, I am currently focused on evolving and maintaining the Big Data infrastructure (Pipeline, Storage, and Query) powering real time analytics, materialized views, and related Reporting and Data Science use cases.

I am a deeply curious individual who enjoys practical learning and creating things from that learning. My personal projects listed here showcase my curiousities and skills. I look at computer-related technologies from a user's perspective, to explore the problems and challenges they can solve. Lastly, I am also a musician/singer-songwriter/music-producer. My solo project has more than 20k listens on Spotify. Please feel free to reach out for anything; I will always respond back :).
